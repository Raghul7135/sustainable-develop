Smart Waste Segregation for Smarter Cities

A low-cost, IoT-powered smart bin that automatically detects, segregates, and reports waste — empowering ULBs with real-time data and cleaner streets.

Partner With Us

🗑️ The Problem: India’s Waste Crisis

India generates 62 million tons of waste annually, but only 30% is segregated at source.

Manual segregation is unhealthy, inefficient, and costly. ULBs spend 40–50% of their sanitation budget just on collection.

Mixed waste leads to overflowing landfills, poor recycling, and health risks for sanitation workers.

Our solution? Make every bin intelligent — so waste is segregated before it’s collected.

💡 The Solution: SmartWaste Bin

Our IoT-enabled smart bin automatically identifies and segregates waste into Wet, Dry, Plastic, and Metal — right at the point of disposal.

Sensor Intelligence

Capacitive, inductive & color sensors detect waste type instantly.

Auto Segregation

Servo-controlled lids route waste to correct compartments.

Real-Time Alerts

Fill-level & weight data sent to ULB dashboard via IoT.

ULB Dashboard

Track bins, optimize routes, and reduce overflow.

🧩 How It Works

Simple, smart, and fully automated.

1

Drop

Citizen drops waste into the bin.

2

Sense

Sensors detect moisture, metal, and color.

3

Decide

Microcontroller classifies the waste.

4

Segregate

Servo opens correct lid automatically.

5

Report

Data sent to cloud dashboard for ULB action.
